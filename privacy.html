  • Generate weight estimates and program recommendations
  • Generate AI-powered program explanations (via Groq Inc.)
  • Enable social features (friend discovery, activity feed)
  • Process subscription payments (via RevenueCat / Apple)

6. Data Sent to AI

When generating program explanations, the following data is sent to Groq Inc.:

  • Training goal, experience level, training frequency
  • Program structure (days, exercises, volume targets)
  • Sex and weight (for contextual recommendations)

This data is transmitted without personal identifiers (no name, email, or user ID).

7. Data Retention

  • Active account: data retained as long as the account is active
  • Account deletion: data deleted within 30 days of request
  • Analytics: anonymized data retained for up to 24 months

8. Your Rights

  • Right of access — obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to object — object to data processing
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact: contact@onsetf.fitness

You may also file a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority (e.g., CNIL in France).

CCPA Rights (California Residents)

Onset Fitness does not sell personal information.

9. Cookies and Tracking

The Application does not use cookies. Device identifiers (IDFA on iOS) may be collected for analytics purposes. You can disable ad tracking in your device settings (Settings > Privacy > Tracking).

10. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

11. Data Security

  • All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Authentication via Supabase (JWT tokens)
  • Local data encrypted by iOS at rest
  • API access protected by authentication and rate limiting

No system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but take reasonable measures to protect your data.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated via the Application. Continued use after notification constitutes acceptance.
