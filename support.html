Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → select Onset Pro → Cancel Subscription.

How do I restore my purchase?

On the paywall screen, tap Restore at the bottom. If you already subscribed, your access will be restored automatically.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app → Settings tab → scroll to the bottom → Delete my account. This will permanently delete all your data.

The app isn't tracking my steps

Onset uses your iPhone's built-in motion sensor. Make sure you've allowed motion access: Settings → Privacy & Security → Motion & Fitness → enable Onset.

My workout data disappeared

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you reinstalled the app, your previous data may be lost. We're working on cloud sync for a future update.
