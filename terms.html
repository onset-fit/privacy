3. TRAINING ESTIMATES AND RECOMMENDATIONS

3.1 Weight Estimates

The Application provides weight estimates based on:

  • Your body weight
  • Population-average statistics (sources: Symmetric Strength, ExRx, Renaissance Periodization)
  • Your training history within the Application

These estimates are approximate starting points, not prescriptions. They:

  • May be too high or too low for your level
  • Do not account for your individual specifics (injuries, build, actual experience)
  • Must be adjusted based on your own judgment and capability

Always start with lighter weights and increase gradually.

3.2 Volume Zones (MV/MEV/MAV/MRV)

Weekly volume zones are based on the work of Renaissance Periodization (Dr. Mike Israetel). These are reference ranges for the general population, not individualized prescriptions. Your optimal volume may differ significantly.

3.3 Progressive Overload

Weight increase recommendations (BUMP/HOLD/DROP) are algorithmic suggestions based on your recent performance. They do not replace your personal judgment.

3.4 Deload Detection

The Application may recommend deload periods when certain muscle groups exceed Maximum Recoverable Volume (MRV) for several consecutive weeks. This detection is automatic and not supervised by a professional.
